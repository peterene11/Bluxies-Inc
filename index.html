Structure and Framework

Bluxies Incorporated prioritizes the individual and collective development of her members; hence, we're looking to partner with organizations that propagate the following values:

Activism

Empowering women to advocate for social change and equality.

Community

Building strong, supportive networks for women to thrive together.

Financial Literacy

Educating women on managing finances for a secure future.

Health and Wellness

Promoting physical and mental well-being.

Mentorship

Connecting women with experienced guides and role models.

Portfolio Building

Assisting in the creation of professional portfolios.

Professional Development

Offering training and resources for career growth.

Self-Improvement

Encouraging continuous personal development.

Skill Acquisition

Providing opportunities to learn new skills.

Women Empowerment

Championing the rights and potential of women.

Volunteering

Encouraging community service and engagement.

Meet Our Founder

Bluxies was created out of two things I love and value: sisterhood and luxury. It is a network of goal-oriented women striving to make something beautiful out of their lives. I’m in certain spaces with high-value and high-caliber women, and I am hoping to replicate the ambiance. We will be exploring everything pertaining to women empowerment. The goal is to build a solid community of well-rounded women who will stand out in society and create sustainable impact.

The community officially launched in October 2023. We currently have 50+ members from different geographical locations, and various events have been held successfully in most of these regions.
