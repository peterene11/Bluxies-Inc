BECOME A VOLUNTEER

Bluxies Incorporated is on the lookout for people who share our vision and are eager to help us create a better future. If you have great work ethics, a willingness to learn, and want to sharpen your skills, then join us.

Become a Part of Our Community

Join our community and help us turn our vision into reality. Volunteering with Bluxies Incorporated gives you the opportunity to grow both personally and professionally.

What's in it for You?

  • A conducive environment to sharpen your skills
  • Personal growth and development
  • Career growth
  • Opportunities for leadership and advancement
  • Valuable relationships

Roles & Responsibilities

Bluxies Designing Team (Visual Content Team)

Being in the Bluxies Designing Team means you get to create cool designs—from flyers to carousels, presentations, and so much more! You're an ideal candidate if you have:

  • 80% Canva expertise
  • Knowledge of other design tools (Figma, CorelDRAW, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, PixelLab, Lightroom, etc.)
  • Knowledge of photo manipulation
  • Strong work ethics
  • Great time management skills

Onboarding Team

The Onboarding Team is quite interesting. You'd get to meet new Bluxies, interview them, and welcome them to the community.

Job Description:

  • Read and review membership applications
  • Interview potential Bluxies
  • Scout for potential members that align with our values

Content Writing Team

The Content Writing Team focuses on creating written material for the organisation, such as social media posts, newsletters, and articles.

Requirements:

  • Strong knowledge of how social media works
  • Basic SEO skills
  • Excellent research skills
  • Effective communication skills
  • Great time management

Fundraising Team

The Fundraising Team is responsible for sourcing potential donors (individuals and organisations) for the organisation.

Job Description:

  • Create compelling pitches and presentations
  • Scout for potential partners
  • Scout for ongoing grants—and win them

To excel in this role, you need:

  • Great writing skills
  • Basic graphic design skills
  • Excellent communication skills

Administrative Team

Job Description:

  • Reach out to potential partners to forge partnerships
  • Oversee events and the organisation’s initiatives
  • Liaise with the Board of Trustees to make decisions for the organisation

Event Planning Team

Job Description:

  • Plan and oversee periodic events in each state
  • Scout potential event locations
  • Handle events’ logistics

Media Team

In the Media Team, you get to create super exciting video content for Bluxies—and have fun doing so!

You're a great fit if you have:

  • Experience in video editing
  • Knowledge of various video editing tools
  • A keen eye for detail
  • A creative mind
  • Bonus points if you're chronically online 😉

How to Apply

Send an email to bluxiesincorporated@gmail.com with the subject: “[Your Name]: Application for [Your Role].” Tell us why you're a great fit and attach samples of your work.

Pro tip: Show your personality – AI-written entries will be automatically disqualified.
